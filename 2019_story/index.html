2019 Story




We rang in the new year with Grandma Arline, Grandma Sheila, Ammumma and Muthachan …

… and got things off to a good start by solving an escape room with Uncle Peter.

Later in January, Emily and Rafe went to witness the start of the viaduct demolition…


… and participated in the Women's March (first of many protests this year).

Before the month was out, we celebrated Grandma Arline's 92nd birthday…

… and Toby and Tate started dating.


In February, Rafe's team took third place in the Mathcounts city competition, and Rafe was the fourth place individual.

February also brought snowmaggedon, aka two weeks of mostly no school but running around with neighborhood kids instead.

Emily escaped snowmaggedon to go to Hawaii for ICLDC (and took some time off during the conference to go meet an octopus!).


In March, Dave visited UW and we got to hang out in person for the first time in 20+ years …

… we celebrated Vijay's birthday with a seafood feast in West Seattle …

… and Emily went to Cambridge for a workshop and, being already across the pond, tacked on quick visits to Edinburgh and Oslo.


In April, we made our annual trek to the tulips in Mt Vernon …

… Toby and Vijay visited Northwestern and its famous school of music …

… and took a trip into the city to see the Second City comedy show.


We celebrated Passover with the Foxes …

… and Toby got the job of being Easter Bunny for Tate's little cousins.

Rafe played for the Hamilton Hawks Ultimate team (here at Spring Reign in Burlington) …


… we celebrated Ammumma and Muthachan's 45th wedding anniversary …

… and Toby's CYSO chamber group was part of the pre-show lobby entertainment at the CYSO spring concert (enabling a close-up photo).

In May, Emily completed her first ever (mini, indoor, time-based) triathlon, and completely unexpectedly won 3rd place (of 9 women competing) …


… Toby turned 17, and wanted a cake but no party …

… we took our annual Mother's Day photos …

… before heading out to Port Townsend to celebrate.


Later that month, Vijay went to Boulder for a Dart summit, where he assures us they did plenty of work.

In June, Toby was part of the pit orchestra for the Ingraham HS production of "Chicago" (where the pit orchestra is on stage) …

… and had his North American debut as a composer, when the Ingraham wind ensemble performed his piece "Daffodil" (recording available here).


Later that month, Rafe finished middle school (with Madeline, a classmate since the baby room at Interlake) …

… and Toby and Tate took a holiday out to Port Townsend.

In July, Rafe went to India with some teachers and classmates …


… and did a service project in a small town near Udaipur.

While Rafe was away, Toby had his wisdom teeth out, Emily completed her first real (outdoor, sprint) triathlon …

… and we celebrated our wedding anniversary with a dinner at the Herb Farm with Amma and Achan.


Later that month, Emily met Rafe in Munich (where his school group was transferring) and brought him to Cambridge for DELPH-IN …

… where we had some time to see the sights, including Newton's apple tree (kind of: this is a graft from the original, near his mother's house, which is also still alive) …

… and then fulfilled Rafe's wish to get to as many countries as possible by traveling by train and spending one night each in Amsterdam (with Antske and Chris) …


… Brussels …

… and Luxembourg, ending in Paris (not a new country for Rafe) and visiting Céline and family before sending Rafe home to Seattle.

Vijay dropped Toby off at Western Washington University for the two-week Marrowstone Music Festival --- college dry run!


After parting with Rafe, Emily went to Pons to visit her host family for a couple of days, and then quickly to Saarbrücken to work with Alexander before heading to …

… Florence for ACL.

At the second week performance at Marrowstone, Toby got to play the contrabassoon (which he first touched at the start of that week).


Meanwhile, Rafe (and Euclid and Euler) stayed with Ammumma and Muthachan for week 1 of CYSO Summer Sounds and Toby joined them for week 2. Here's a rare photo of both kids performing in the same ensemble!

Later in August, Rafe went to Orkila for LDI …

… and then right back with the rest of us for family camp!


While we were there, we took Toby's senior photo.

Then it was September and the first day of 12th and 9th grade (two highschoolers!) …

… and a quick trip for Emily to CA to be a panelist at WeCNLP and also give a talk at Google and visit with Susanne …


… a trip to Aarhus for Vijay …

… and Rafe's birthday, celebrated with a school day lunch at Chutney's and an escape room party on the weekend.

This year, Toby tried to disprove Rafe's claim that any foods he likes to eat he'd like to eat together, but so far has only managed to gross himself out.


Later in September, cousin Allison visited and we had a mini Bender cousins get together …

… Tate asked Toby to homecoming …

In December, Emily and Vijay enjoyed some fake snow at the Google holiday party …
